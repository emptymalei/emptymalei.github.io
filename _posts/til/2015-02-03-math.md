## Feynman trick

The following identity is true,

$$
\frac{1}{AB} = \int_0^1 \frac{1}{[A + (B - A) x ]^2} dx .
$$


## Area enclosed by a line

Area enclosed by a line is given by 

.. math::
   \vec S = \frac{1}{2}\int \vec x \times \vec{d l}.
